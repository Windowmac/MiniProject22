Type O Negative Kicks Ass

This is a fact. From their very first album (Slow, Deep, and Hard) to their last (Dead Again), every iteration kicked even more ass.

Reasons Type O kicks ass include (but are not limited to):

  • The band went platinum with 1993's Bloody Kisses, and gold with 1996's October Rust

  • They have produced 7 studio albums, 2 best-of compilations and a live DVD

  • The band's blend of gothic, death and romance earned them the moniker "the Drab Four"

  • Peter Steele (the band's now-deceased frontman) appeared in Playgirl's 1995 (Aug) issue (obviously very badass)
